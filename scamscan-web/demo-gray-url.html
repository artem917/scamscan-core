Demo / training page

Networked AI portfolio manager

This demo landing page is intentionally written to look like a high-yield product without being a direct scam. It is hosted by ScamScan and does not advertise any real service.

Our automated trading bot connects to multiple exchanges through a single investment platform interface. The goal is to generate passive income over time while the system rebalances your portfolio in the background.

Users choose from several experimental strategies with different risk levels. Returns are variable and depend on market conditions; results are not guaranteed and balances can go down as well as up.

Social layer

Members can share access codes with people they trust. When invited contacts activate a strategy, the original member may receive a small fee share — essentially a way to invite friends and earn together inside the same ecosystem.

Why this page exists

Many borderline products combine terms such as investment platform, trading bot, passive income and invite friends and earn to suggest low-effort profit without clearly explaining the risks. ScamScan uses pages like this as gray-area examples for testing content risk models and explaining why a URL may look risky even if it is not a classic credential-stealing scam.
